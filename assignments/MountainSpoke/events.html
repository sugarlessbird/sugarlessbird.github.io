Cycle the Continental Divide Annual Event!





This year marks the 13th annual Cycle The Coninental Divide event!

Join us for games, food and prizes as we test who has the best time in the Divide.

This tour will take you out of the city and into the great wilderness of the mountains around Bozeman. Plan on a half day (4 hrs) of biking.

Bring lots of water and sunscreen. Trail cameras will record your tour and broadcast to the audience at the town square!

Whoever cycles the Divide in the best time wins! Last year the Grand Prize was a full tour for FREE!
